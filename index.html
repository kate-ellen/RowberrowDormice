Survey Sign Up

If you'd like to sign up to be a volunteer surveyor, you will need to register as a Forestry England volunteer.

1. Complete this form.

2. When asked which forest you'd like to volunteer in you'll find that 'Rowberrow Warren' isn't available. Select 'Long Ashton' instead.

3. Inform the lead surveyors that you have completed the form using the contact details below. If you don't complete this step, your registration will not progress.

4. Forestry England will then review your form, and send out a second form.

5. Complete the second form, you will then receive a QR code. Once the QR code is received you're ready to go.

FAQs

Q: How many people can attend a survey?
A: Usually 6 including the lead surveyor, if we have an extra person with a licence we may extend this to 8.

Q: Can I bring my children to the survey?
A: We are not insured or trained to accomodate children on the surveys.

Q: Do surveys go ahead regardless of the weather?
A: Surveys may be cancelled in high winds, heavy rain or very high temperatures for the welfare of dormice and safety of volunteers.
